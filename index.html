Government Schemes

Tourism and economic support programs for sustainable development

Jharkhand Tourism Development Scheme

Infrastructure Development

Development of tourist circuits, infrastructure improvement, and promotion of eco-tourism destinations across the state.

  • Road connectivity improvement
  • Tourist facility centers
  • Digital promotion campaigns
  • Skill development for local guides
Budget: ₹500 Crores

Tribal Handicraft Promotion Scheme

Artisan Support

Supporting local artisans and craftspeople by providing market access, training, and financial assistance.

  • Direct market linkage
  • Quality certification
  • Digital marketplace access
  • Skill enhancement workshops
Budget: ₹150 Crores

Rural Homestay Development Program

Community Tourism

Encouraging rural families to start homestay businesses with subsidies and training support.

  • 50% subsidy on setup costs
  • Hospitality training programs
  • Online booking platform
  • Marketing support
Up to ₹2 Lakhs per family

Sustainable Eco-Tourism Initiative

Environmental Conservation

Promoting responsible tourism practices while conserving natural resources and supporting local communities.

  • Conservation project funding
  • Community guide training
  • Waste management systems
  • Wildlife protection programs
Budget: ₹300 Crores

Digital Tourism Platform Initiative

Technology Integration

Creating digital infrastructure for online bookings, virtual tours, and smart tourism management.

  • Mobile app development
  • Virtual reality tours
  • AI-powered recommendations
  • Multi-language support
Budget: ₹200 Crores

MSME Tourism Support Scheme

Business Development

Financial and technical support for small and medium enterprises in the tourism sector.

  • Low-interest loans
  • Business development support
  • Technology adoption assistance
  • Market expansion help
Up to ₹50 Lakhs per unit
